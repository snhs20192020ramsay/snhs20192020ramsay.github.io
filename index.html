Advocacies for Change

The Grade - 12 Ramsay Students presently under the subject empowerment technology that guides students to better use technology through its competencies are encouraged to promote advocacies which direct audience to be aware of the present situations that the community is undertaking. With the support and expertise of their teacher, Mr. Arnel G. Calaro, the whole section has formulated different advocacies that promotes change in the society.

Sarrat National High School

The school aims to develop and produce students who are technology ready for the welfare of the community. It further seeks to explore new horizons in technology at it develops the skills of students necessary for future development.

Linking the School and Community

The school as a training ground to students focuses on the development of students that would be beneficial to the community especially to the environment. Our community's goal to develop the economy is one with the goal of the school to produce international recognized skills of students.
